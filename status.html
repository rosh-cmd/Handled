Status Realty needed a brand that could support how the business would actually operate — across marketing, sales materials, and day-to-day communication. The goal wasn't just to create a strong identity, but to ensure it could be seamlessly applied consistently across everything the business produced. Without that, the brand would quickly fragment as output increased.

We developed a full brand identity alongside a structured set of templates and core materials, designed for real-world use, not just presentation. This included: A clear visual identity and brand system + Templates for marketing, sales, and internal use + Guidelines that could be applied consistently across different outputs.

Following the initial rollout, I continued to support ongoing brand management and production, ensuring the brand was applied consistently as the business grew. The focus wasn't just on creating the brand, but on making sure it held over time.

The brand remained consistent across all touchpoints from marketing materials to day-to-day communication. Work could be produced more efficiently using the established templates and system. And new materials aligned naturally without needing to be rethought each time. Instead of drifting, the brand strengthened as more output was created.
