Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Handled Studio is a design production practice operated by Rosh Java, based in Australia. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: rosh@handledstudio.com.au

What we collect

When you book a Fit Check or contact us

We collect your name, email address, and any information you provide in your message or booking form. This information is used solely to schedule and conduct our conversation with you.

When you subscribe to our newsletter

We collect your email address. This is used to send you occasional updates about Handled Studio. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

When you visit our website

Our website may collect standard analytics data through cookies, including pages visited, time on site, and referring source. This data is anonymised and used to understand how visitors interact with our site. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

How we use your information

We use personal information only for the purpose it was provided: to respond to enquiries, schedule calls, deliver services, and send communications you have opted into.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We use the following third-party services that may process your data: our website hosting provider, Calendly (scheduling), and any email marketing platform we use for newsletters. Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We only use services that comply with applicable data protection standards.

Your personal information is stored securely through the platforms listed above. We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. We do not store credit card or payment information on our systems.

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: rosh@handledstudio.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

Updates to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website with the date of last update.
