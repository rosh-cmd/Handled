Most clients are producing somewhere between 20 and 30 assets a month... listings, appraisal kits, EDMs, social content, signage, campaign pieces. Some months heavier, some lighter. That's normal.

There's nothing to scope and nothing to true up. The queue absorbs your rhythm... busy months just mean a fuller queue, and slow months you can simply pause. Questions worth asking before you decide

One asset, one purpose. A DL flyer counts as one request [both sides]. Social graphics up to five tiles on one theme count as one. Signage is counted per sign design. Every client gets one priority bump a month. Auction Saturday doesn't wait, and we've built for that.
